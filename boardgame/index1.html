Game Instructions

1.Press the start button to enter in to the game.

2.Enter names of player1 and player2.

3.Enter numbers to opponent player has to guess it.

4.After entering details click submit details button

5.Enter 0 to 9 numbers in each box.

6.The box can take only single digit.

7.Now check how many numbers written by you as correct by hitting submit button.

8.On each submission no of attempts will be increased.

9.If you want to replay game click retake button to restart the game.
